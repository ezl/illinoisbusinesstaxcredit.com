Do I Qualify?

The key eligibility requirements are as follows:

  1. You must have created a job on or after July 1st, 2012
  2. This job must be a full-time position
  3. This job must pay minimum wage or greater
  4. This job position must have been filled for 12 months
  5. Your business must have less than 50 employees

 

For each job you've created that qualifies, you can receive a $2500 tax credit directly from the Illinois Department of Revenue.

Does your small business have jobs that qualify? If so, talk to one of our specialists so we can help you register for a credit immediately.

We offer a 100% money back guarantee and we NEVER charge you UNLESS you receive your tax credit. If you do not receive the credit, you will never be charged.

For each $2500 of tax credits you receive, we charge a $495 processing and application fee.
